Our Story

Welcome to Aruna Textiles & Nighties, your home for comfortable and elegant nightwear, born right here in the heart of Suryapet, Telangana. Our journey began with a simple belief: that every woman deserves to end her day feeling relaxed, comfortable, and beautiful.

From Our Family to Yours

We are a family-run business that has been part of the Suryapet community for years. We noticed that it was often difficult to find nightwear that was both high-quality and designed for the real needs and preferences of Indian women. We wanted to create pieces that weren't just for sleeping, but for living in—for those quiet moments reading a book, enjoying a cup of tea, or relaxing with family.

Each piece in our collection is chosen with care. We focus on soft, breathable fabrics, flattering fits that offer ease of movement, and designs that are both modern and timeless. We understand our customers because we are part of the same community.

Our Promise

Our promise is simple: to provide you with beautiful, comfortable nightwear that you'll love to wear, night after night. Thank you for supporting a local Suryapet business and for letting us be a small part of your comfort.
