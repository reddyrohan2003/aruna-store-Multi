Our Story

Welcome to Aruna Textiles & Nighties, your home for comfortable and elegant nightwear in the heart of Suryapet, Telangana. We are a family-run business dedicated to providing high-quality, breathable fabrics that are perfect for the Indian climate.

Our journey began over a decade ago with a simple mission: to create nightwear that isn’t just for sleeping, but for living. We wanted to design pieces that make you feel relaxed and confident, whether you're winding down after a long day, enjoying your morning tea, or spending a lazy weekend at home.

Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully selected, keeping in mind the need for practical sizing (from M to 3XL), easy care, and timeless designs. We believe in the personal touch, which is why we've built our online experience around a simple and direct WhatsApp chat. When you order from us, you're talking to family.

Thank you for supporting our local business. We hope you find something you love.
